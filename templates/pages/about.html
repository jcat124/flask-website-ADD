ABOUT ME

My name is Jora Singh Nahal; I am a grade 7 student at an Elementary School in BC, Canada. I have a strong passion for STEM and sports, which is evident from the fact that I have won 11 prestigious STEM awards at the national and regional levels, and more than a dozen awards in multiple sports. I am an active member of the leadership committee at my Elementary School, and I also enjoy spending fun time with my family and friends.

I have been doing Regional Science Fairs since grade 4 where I have won two gold medals, two best-category awards—Best Novice Project Award twice, and was featured in sd73 news: https://www.sd73.bc.ca/Modules/News/index.aspx?newsId=8e51b4aa-4adb-45e2-8881-4a79421ac9f6

And, since grade 2, I have also been participating and have won 7 regional and national awards in three different regional and national-level math competitions: Math Kangaroo Canada, Mathematica Centrum, and District Math Challenge. I was featured in the local news: https://infotel.ca/newsitem/are-you-smarter-than-this-third-grader-kamloops-whiz-kid-takes-home-gold-in-math-competition/it53353

I love sports too. I am a multi-sport athlete. I have played soccer for 5 years; I am also on my school’s team for basketball, volleyball, track and field, and cross country. And I have won more than a dozen sports awards.

I also like spending fun time with my family and friends. I like mountain biking, hiking, playing video games and board games, and watching movies with my family. I play a musical instrument called Harmonium and am also learning to play Tabla.
